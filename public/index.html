Talking

A project by Ishac Bertran and Jonathan Wohl

Talking investigates the mechanics of experience, expression, and interaction. Using computer-vision techniques to design single-sense computing devices, we construct a framework for thinking about the processes involved in perception and communication.

We've developed two parts of this project, each exploring different aspects of the same setup: Two identical devices, consisting of camera, computer, and screen, are positioned facing one another, allowing each to 'see' and 'talk' to the other.
